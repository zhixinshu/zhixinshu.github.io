About

I'm a research scientist at Adobe Research. Between 2013 to 2019, I was a PhD student at Stony Brook University advised by Professor Dimitris Samaras.

I'm interested in research topics related to human in the intersection of computer vision, computer graphics and machine learning. My recent work has a focus on human face centered image understanding, generation, and editing.

Feel free to email me if you are interested in my work, have exciting ideas to chat about and collaborate on, or looking for research internships at Adobe.
